el' Nynaeve ti al'Meara Mandragoran

Queen of Malkier and Yellow Ajah

Intro

Healer with a passion for learning and strong leadership skills. Raised in the Two Rivers learning woodscraft at a young age, I was apprenticed to the local Wisdom where I began my passion for the healing arts. I left for the White Tower in my young twenties as a Wilder following my own apprentice when a Blue sister came into town meddleing.

Skills

  • Healing of every kind
  • Workshops to teach advanced healing techniques
  • Weather prediction
  • Weather intervention (require's local government approval)
  • Mild affinity for deciphering Ter'angreal use

Notable Achievements

  • Youngest Wisdom ever in Two Rivers
  • Entered White Tower as a Wilder with enough skill to start as Accepted
  • First channeler to heal Stilling and Gentleing
  • Worked with the Dragon Reborn to cleanse the taint on Saidin
  • Organized the military forces of 12,000 men for Tarwin's Gap under the banner of the Golden Crane
